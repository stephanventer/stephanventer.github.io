Privacy Policy for Transonic

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Vonk Limited ("we", "us", "our"), developed Transonic (the "Game"), a free 3D arcade endless runner for Android. This policy explains what data the Game collects, how it's used, and who it's shared with.

Data We Collect

1. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

The Game shows rewarded video ads via Google AdMob to let players revive after death. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, IP-derived approximate location)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, completions)

This data is used for ad delivery, frequency capping, and fraud prevention. See Google's Privacy & Terms and AdMob's data disclosure.

2. Google Play Games Services (GPGS)

If you sign in with Google Play Games, the Game accesses:

  • Your Play Games player display name
  • Your public leaderboard scores (submitted to the game's online leaderboard)

Sign-in is optional. If you decline or are offline, the Game falls back to a local top-10 leaderboard stored only on your device.

3. Crash & Diagnostic Data (Sentry)

The Game uses Sentry to capture crash reports and error logs to help us fix bugs. This may include device model, OS version, stack traces, and anonymized session/run identifiers. No personally identifying information is intentionally included.

4. Locally Stored Data

The following stays on your device only, unless synced via GPGS as noted above:

  • Local top-10 leaderboard (user://scores.json)
  • Gameplay stats (runs played, best score, time played, revives used, ads watched)
  • Session/run identifiers (randomly generated, not tied to your identity)

How We Use Data

  • Operate and improve the Game
  • Display and measure ads
  • Diagnose crashes and bugs
  • Show leaderboards and rankings

We do not sell your personal data.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses these third-party SDKs, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Games Services
  • Sentry

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. AdMob is configured to serve non-personalized/family-safe ads where applicable under Google Play's Families policies.

Data Retention & Deletion

Local data is deleted when you uninstall the Game or clear its app data. To request deletion of GPGS-linked leaderboard data, contact us below or manage your data via your Google Account.

Your Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device's ad settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • You can decline Google Play Games sign-in and use the Game with local-only leaderboards.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the Game evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
